# Design Rules

Preserve:
- Existing color palette
- Existing page layouts
- Typography hierarchy
- Hero sections
- Card structure

Improve:
- Hover animations
- Transition consistency
- Spacing consistency
- Button interactions

Localization:
Convert company content to Indian standards.

Examples:

Singapore HQ → Bengaluru HQ
London Office → Pune Office

Sarah Chen → Dr. Aditi Kulkarni
Marcus Reid → Rohan Deshmukh

Replace:

"Get Started"

with:

"Request Demo"

Avoid military terminology.

Replace:

"Defense-grade security"

with:

"Enterprise Healthcare Security"

Replace:

"Military-grade sandbox"

with:

"Secure Clinical Data Isolation"
